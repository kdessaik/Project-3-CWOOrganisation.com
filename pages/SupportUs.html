About CWOOrganization

CARING FOR WIDOWS AND ORPHANS ORGANIZATION(CWOO) is a non-benefit community based organization that aims at empowering orphans, widows and elderly people with special needs. This has been founded in Kakuma refugee camp Turkana west back in January 2018 as an initiative from people of good will passionate on assisting the community

Our Vision

  1. sure that we at least help a good number of vulnerable youths and people with special needs to a larger target.
  2. Having a sustainable orphanage center that would be there to assists homeless children and orphans, and making a training computer center for them.
  3. Being the voices of voiceless people and help the community at large.

Our Mission

We are aiming at becoming a big organization with full funds that will be able to expand our services beyond Africa and worldwide and to reach out and help widows, orphans and other vulnerable groups in world.
