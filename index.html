Projects

CWOO humanitarian

CARING FOR WIDOWS AND ORPHANS ORGANIZATION(CWOOrg) is a non-benefit community based organization that aims at empowering orphans, widows and elderly people with special needs. This has been founded in Kakuma refugee camp Turkana west back in January 2018 as an initiative from people of good will passionate on assisting the community

Our Activities

Foods' Donative

With a small ration in camp receiving monthly , a widow of 8 children can not assist all these children,so we contribute excess foods for them(widows).

Training

We train Widows in camp and host community to become self dependant in our tailoring projet

Computer Class

We train orphans in camp and host community to use a computer, so that they become familiar with the new technology, we do this in our computer class project
